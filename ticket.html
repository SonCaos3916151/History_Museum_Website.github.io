Ticket costs & regulations

Below is our ticket fees and regulations at the museum

 

ADMISSION FEE AT THE MUSEUM (Effective from January 1, 2018)

 A. PRICE:40,000VND/PAX

B. PRICE POLICIES:

           I. Free entry is applied for the following cases:

                    1. Children under 6 years old.

                   2. People with extremely severe disabilities as stipulated in Clause 1–Article 11 of the Government’s Decree No.28/2012/NĐ-CP of April 10, 2012.

                    3. Poor households as stipulated in the Prime Minister’s Decision No. 59/2015/QĐ-TTg of  November 19, 2015.

           II. 50 percent of concessions are available to the following groups:

                    1. Children aged 6 - 15.

                    2. Vietnamese citizens aged 60 and above as stipulated in Article 2 of the Law on the Elderly.

                   3. People with severe disabilities asstipulated in Clause 2 - Article 11 of the Government’s Decree No. 28/2012/NĐ-CP of April 10, 2012.

                   4. People who receive preferential treatment on cultural enjoyment in Article 2 of the Prime Minister’s Decision No. 170/2003/QĐ-TTg of August 14, 2003, including:

                * Inhabitants in extremely poor, mountainous and remote communes as stipulated in the Government’s Program 135.

                * People with meritorious service to the Vietnamese revolutions:

                          a) Cadres who long engaged in national revolution.

                          b) Martyrs’relatives.

                          c) The Heroes of the People’s Armed Forces, Labor Heroes and Heroic Vietnamese Mothers.

                          d) War invalids and people who enjoy policies aimed for war invalids and diseased veterans.

                          e) People who are tended at centers for war invalids, diseased veterans and people with meritorious service.

                 * Beneficiaries of social policies:

                         a) The handicapped, the elderly who live alone.

                         b) People who are tended at social relief centers.

                         c) Students at boarding schools for ethnic minorities.

Note: Those who are allowed free entries or concessions should produce required documents in accordance with relevant laws.
