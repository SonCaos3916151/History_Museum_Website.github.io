Since 1975, the War Remnant Museum
was created to exposing War Crimes that
happend to Vietnam in since 1954.

Operated by the Ho Chi Minh City government, an earlier version of this museum opened on September 4, 1975, as the Exhibition House for US and Puppet Crimes. It was located in the former United States Information Agency building. The exhibition was created to exposing war crimes, first those of the French and then those of the Americans, who had operated in the country as early as 1954.
